The London Consent Project is a not-for-profit, lawyer-run initiative, which provides one hour workshops in schools for both boys and girls to explore the legal framework surrounding sexual offences and consent.

The London Consent Project in numbers:

  • 43% of students had never discussed consent before a London Consent Project workshop
  • 86% of students felt more empowered to take informed decisions about consent after a London Consent Project workshop
  • 86% of students found the content of the London Consent Project workshop easily accessible
  • 90% of students enjoyed the interactive nature of the London Consent Project workshop
  • 94% of students felt that the workshop hosts (hyperlink to ‘Who We Are’) delivered the London Consent Project workshop in an approachable and engaging manner
  • 96% of students felt that the workshop hosts (hyperlink to ‘Who We Are’) responded well to their questions
*statistics based on a survey of 49 students
